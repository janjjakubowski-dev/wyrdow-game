\f0\fs24 \cf0 # Game Bible \'97 Wyrd\'f3w (Working Title)\
\
## World\
Post-apocalyptic. Old Slavic and Chasidic folklore has become real after a collapse that fractured the world's memory.\
The boundary between science and superstition has blurred \'97 old rites actually work, though no one fully understands why.\
The Regency bans all folk tradition, calling them "Legacy Myths."\
The player is an ordinary person searching for a Cure that was intentionally erased.\
\
## Tone\
Heroic, lighthearted, but with emotional weight.\
Whimsical on the surface, dystopian underneath.\
Think: wonder mixed with grief. Folk fairy tale meets quiet resistance.\
\
## Starting Location \'97 Wyrd\'f3w\
A crossroads village where five overgrown roads meet at a straw-and-iron idol.\
Crooked wooden houses lean inward. Every house has equal windows on both sides.\
Protected by Po\uc0\u322 udnica (the noonwraith) \'97 revered, not feared. Midday silence is sacred.\
Locals wear charms of broken glass and poppy seeds.\
Disused wayside shrines glow at dusk for unknown reasons.\
The roads sometimes rearrange at night unless you carry blue thread soaked in wine.\
\
## Key NPC \'97 Baba Elzbieta\
Prickly matriarch of the Stitched Circle (local herbalists and folklore-keepers).\
Can read "twisted fates" from a burnt braid.\
She is the player's first quest giver.\
\
## Main Quest Hook\
The player awakens in Wyrd\'f3w with a mysterious cure-sign glowing on their hand.\
They must find fragments of the Cure Codex, scattered across five towns.\
The first fragment is hidden inside a woven amulet in Wyrd\'f3w.\
The quest to find it involves realigning the crossroads using enchanted thread knots.\
\
## Visual Style\
Isometric top-down view. Earthy, muted palette \'97 ochre, moss green, ash grey, deep red.\
Folk-art inspired. Slightly eerie. Candlelit and dusk-toned.\
Western Slavic aesthetic \'97 wooden longhouses, wayside shrines, dirt paths, copper details.\
\
## Tech Stack\
Phaser.js for the game engine.\
Single HTML file unless told otherwise.\
Browser-based \'97 no install required for players.\
Keep code clean and commented so a non-technical person can follow along.\

\outl0\strokewidth0 \strokec2 ## Atmosphere\
- Visual: folk illustration damaged by rain. Tarnished gold, ash white, ember orange, bruised purple (Regency)\
- Lighting: always candlelit, dusk, or moonwashed. No clean daylight.\
- Music: sparse. Hurdy-gurdy, hammered dulcimer, half-remembered melodies.\
- Writing voice: poetic but never pretentious. Survivors who carry things quietly.\
\
## The Regency\
Not a villain faction \'97 bureaucratic dread. Soft voices, clipboards, forms that erase people.\
Their aesthetic is cold, clean, metallic \'97 a stark contrast to every town.\
\
## Act 1 Beat Structure\
1. Player wakes at edge of Wyrd\'f3w with glowing mark on hand\
2. Child NPC points them to Baba Elzbieta\
3. Baba Elzbieta reads their fate, sends them to fix the crossroads\
4. Player finds 5 markers, places thread knots, receives visions\
5. Roads straighten. Po\uc0\u322 udnica appears briefly.\
6. Baba Elzbieta reveals the first Cure Codex fragment\
7. A Wellness Officer arrives as player leaves. Act 1 ends.\
